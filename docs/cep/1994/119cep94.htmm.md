<p class=MsoBodyText style='text-indent:36.0pt'><b>§ 2</b><b><span

<p class=MsoBodyText style='text-indent:36.0pt'><b>Art. 31.</b> São deveres dos

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span
