<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>2&nbsp;-&nbsp;Ser

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

ato da matrícula, conforme o disposto no artigo 8</span><span style='font-size:

Física, nos termos do &quot;caput&quot; deste artigo, respectivamente: <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span
style='font-size:12.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial'>- Se

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

vencimento.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span

<p class=MsoNormal style='text-align:justify;text-indent:36.0pt'><span
